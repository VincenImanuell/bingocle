# Introduction

**Bingocle** = **Bing**o + Or**acle** — a community bingo prediction market where the
entire game loop is run and refereed by **AI agents on the Mantle Network**.

The audience of any live event — a conference, podcast, debate, livestream, or demo
day — proposes words they predict a speaker will say. AI curates those words into a
pool and into bingo cards, each word becomes an on-chain prediction asset traded on a
bonding curve, and an **AI Validation Oracle listens to the event live (speech-to-text
+ LLM) and writes its verdicts straight to a smart contract**. That single on-chain
write triggers settlement, card marking, and reward distribution — automatically and
transparently.

No admin marks words by hand. No black box. Every AI decision is recorded permanently
on-chain — embodying the hackathon's Turing Test theme: on-chain benchmarking of AI,
agent identity, and radical transparency.

## Two coupled deliverables

Bingocle ships as two halves that share the same contracts:

1. **Bingocle on-chain** — the trust + innovation layer. Seven Solidity contracts on
   Mantle, the AI Speech Oracle, and a public Next.js web app.
2. **Bingocle Capability** — the consumer surface. A Minds Bazaar skill that lets
   anyone create or join an event, submit words, buy positions, watch live, and claim —
   entirely through chat over Telegram (and email).

## How the pieces fit

| Layer | What lives here | Stack |
|---|---|---|
| **Smart contracts** | `EventFactory`, `WordPool`, `WordMarket`, `OracleRegistry`, `BingoCardNFT`, `RewardVault`, `AgentIdentity` | Solidity 0.8.28 · Foundry · OpenZeppelin |
| **Agent service** | AI Word Curator, Odds Engine, Validation Oracle + an HTTP API and on-chain signer | Node/TS · Express · ethers v6 · Gemini `2.5-flash` |
| **Capability** | Telegram bot, natural-language understanding, per-user demo wallets | Telegraf · ethers · Gemini |
| **Frontend** | Landing page, a client-side demo, and the live on-chain market | Next.js · wagmi/viem · RainbowKit |

The agent wallet is registered as the **ERC-8004 oracle identity** and as the
`WordPool` curator, so one AI identity both curates the pool and commits live verdicts —
with its accuracy accruing permanently to a soulbound reputation NFT.

## What makes it different

- **A speech oracle as a market primitive.** Settlement is driven by *words actually
  spoken in a live event* — not price feeds or political outcomes. No existing oracle
  (Chainlink, UMA, Polymarket) addresses this.
- **Solvent by construction.** Trading on the bonding curve is fee-free, so the market
  balance always equals its total reserve. The oracle only decides *who* wins — never
  whether funds exist to pay them.
- **Portable AI reputation.** The oracle agent is a soulbound ERC-8004 identity NFT.
  Validations, events served, and disputes upheld are written on-chain and travel with
  the agent.
